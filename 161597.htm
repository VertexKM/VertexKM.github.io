About your activity and notifications

The Activity tab lists all activity related to your profile in Teams. When you view the Activity tab, the following options are available:

  • Feed - This includes activity from your profile as well as other users' interactions with your activity, such as responses to posts.
  • My Activity - This lists all of your direct actions across all of the Teams of which you are a member. It does not include activity from other members.

You can control the types of notifications you receive about activity in a channel or the site. This enables you to limit or silence channels that are of lower importance or relevance and highlight activity that should be a priority. You can configure the following types of activities:

  • All new posts - You receive notification whenever there is a new post in the channel.
  • Include all replies - You receive a notification for all replies to a post in the channel.
  • Channel mentions - You receive a notification whenever the channel is mentioned.

When you configure activity notification, you choose from the following options:

  • Banner and feed - You receive notification as a desktop message and an alert in your activity feed.
  • Only show in feed - You receive notification as an alert in your activity feed.
  • Off - You do not receive any notifications for the channel.

You can change the notifications you receive in the Activity tab and elsewhere in Teams. This includes the following locations:

  • Channels
  • Posts
  • Additional settings in your user profile

If needed, you can turn notifications on or off for specific discussion threads in channel posts. This enables you to reduce distractions if a conversation does not affect you. When you turn off notifications for a post, you will still receive a notification if you are mentioned.

In addition to changing notifications in specific areas of the site, you can also apply broader changes from your user profile. This enables you to change notification settings for the following areas:

  • Mentions
  • Messages
  • Other
  • Highlights for you
