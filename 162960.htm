Working with FAQ items

Complete the following to work with FAQ items:

  1. (Optional) To add an FAQ item, do the following:
    1. On the FAQ list page, click New in the tool bar at the top. The New item drawer opens.
    2. In the Question field, type the question.
    3. In the Answer field, type the answer.
    4. To display the question and answer at the top of the list, click the Sticky slider to Yes.
    5. To display the question and answer just below the Sticky question, click the Featured slider to Yes.

      Note: Do not select Featured if you selected Sticky for the item.

    6. In the Category field, type a category for the question.
    7. Click Save.
  2. (Optional) To edit an FAQ item, do the following:
    1. On the FAQ list page, select the radio button next to the question to edit.
    2. In the tool bar at the top, click Edit. The Item properties drawer opens.
    3. Make changes to the item as needed.
    4. Click Save.
  3. (Optional) To delete an FAQ item, do the following:
    1. On the FAQ list page, select the radio button next to the question to delete.
    2. In the tool bar at the top, click Delete.
  4. (Optional) To share an FAQ item, do the following:
    1. On the FAQ list page, select the radio button next to the question to share.
    2. In the tool bar at the top, click Share. The Send Link dialog box opens.
    3. Use the top drop-down to select the type of access required to open the link.
    4. In the Enter a name or email address field, enter the recipient's name or email address.
    5. (Optional) Enter a message in the Add a message (optional) field.
    6. Click Send. You can also click Copy Link and paste the link into an instant message or email.
