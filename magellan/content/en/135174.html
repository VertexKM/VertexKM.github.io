Internal example: Reporting Purge: RDBVatReturnsExportStatus table

Problem

If a customer runs a Reporting Purge in O Series 7.0 or 8.0, and the RDBVatReturnsExportStatus table is not purged during the process, the customer may experience an issue when the Transaction Data Upgrade Utility is run for O Series 7.0 to 8.0 and O Series 8.0 to 9.0.

Solution

To diagnose this issue, check the RDBVatReturnsExportStatus table and ensure that all lineItems that are used in the lineItemId column exist in the RBDLineItemRef table. Also, remove any items in the RDBVatReturnsExportStatus table that do not have corresponding items in the RBDLineItemRef table.
