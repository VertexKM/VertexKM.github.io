Understanding knowledge article creation

These guidelines are intended for content that is posted to myVertex (Oracle Service Cloud) and to Vertex Cloud (WordPress). If additional hosting for knowledge articles is available at a later date, the publishing sections in this guide will be updated to reflect the new output capabilities. For foundational products, such as Q and L Series, continue to use the current writing standards.

Goals of a knowledge article:

  • Empower users to self-serve to find resolutions to their issues

  • Help Customer Support representatives quickly provide users with answers

  • Ensure consistent answers to issues
