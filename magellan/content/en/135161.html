External example: How can I move historical Zip9 data to my O Series 9.0 deployment?

All full data updates for Vertex® Indirect Tax O Series® contain historical data for TaxGIS, jurisdictional tax rules, and jurisdictional tax rates. The historical data is cumulative from the beginning of the Vertex O Series product (2003). However, the full data update contains only a single year of data for nine-digit ZIP Codes (Zip9). You can build historical Zip9 data over time when you apply updates to your previous version of O Series and upgrade to your new version of O Series.

When you run the O Series upgrade utility, one of the steps displays the Zip9 Data Selection page with the following option: Would you like to perform US ZIP9-based lookups more than one year in the past? This option is not selected by default.

Select this option to move all Zip9 records, including older and expired Zip9 data, to your 9.0 deployment from the source database (the previous version of O Series). You can then look up and process transactions that have a tax date of more than one year in the past and specify addresses that use nine-digit postal codes. If you do not need to process these types of transactions, do not select the Zip9 option.

note>When you upgrade from a previous version of O Series, the only way to provide historical Zip9 data in the new version of O Series is to select the Zip9 option in the upgrade utility. If you do not select this option, no records are moved during the upgrade.
