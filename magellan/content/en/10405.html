About this guide

This guide contains information for the system administrator, network administrator, and database administrator (DBA), who collectively implement, maintain, and manage O Series users, security, and data.

The system administrator should be familiar with hardware and software implementation, network management, databases, and security. The network administrator should have the skills to address O Series-related security activities that occur outside of O Series. The database administrator should have experience in setting up and managing databases.

This guide describes what you need to know to do the following:

  • Assemble your O Series administration team

  • Transition effectively from installation to administration activities

  • Manage security

  • Manage O Series data and apply data updates

  • Monitor system performance

  • Troubleshoot issues

  • Understand Tax Area IDs and perform Tax Area lookups
