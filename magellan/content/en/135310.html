Working with channels (Administration)

Note: This topic is intended for site administrators.

To work with channels, do the following:

  1. To add a channel, do the following:

    1. Click the ellipsis next to the team and select Add channel.

    2. Enter a name and description of the channel.

    3. Use the Privacy drop-down list to select whether the channel is public or private.

    4. Click Add.

  2. To add members to a private channel, do the following:

    1. Click the ellipsis next to the channel and select Manage channel.

    2. On the Manage channel page, click Add member.

    3. Start typing the member's name and select the member from the results list.

    4. Add as many members as needed.

    5. Click Add.

    6. Click Close. The members are automatically added as standard Members to the channel.

  3. To change a member's role in a private channel, do the following:

    1. Click the ellipsis next to the channel and select Manage channel.

    2. Locate the user and select the Role drop-down next to their name.

    3. Select Owner or Member, depending on the change required.

  4. To remove members from a private channel, do the following:

    1. If the member is an Owner, change the role to Member.

    2. In the Members and guests list, locate the member.

    3. In the Role column, select the X to remove the member.
