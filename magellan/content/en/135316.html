Working with team communications

Complete the following to communicate with a team:

  1. To chat with a channel, do the following in the desired channel:

    1. Type your message in the Start a new conversation field.

    2. Click Send ().

    3. To reply to a chat, click Reply, type your message, and click Send.

  2. To work with @mentions, do the following:

    1. To direct a message to a specific team member, type @<member> in the chat and select the team member from the pop-up list that displays.

    2. To direct a message to everyone who is part of a channel, type @<channel> in the channel chat and select the channel name from the pop-up list that displays.

    3. To direct a message to everyone who is part of a team, type @<team> in the chat and select the team name from the pop-up list that displays.

  3. To work with channel emails, do the following:

    1. Click the ellipsis next to the channel and select Get email address.

    2. Click Copy.

    3. In Outlook, paste the address into the To field.

    4. Complete and send the email.

    5. (Optional) To open the original email, click View original email and click OK when prompted to open the file. The email opens in Outlook

  4. To chat with a single user, do the following:

    1. In the Teams left navigation column, click Chat ().

    2. Use the search bar to find the contact or, in the Chat pane, select the contact.

    3. Type your message and click Send.

    4. To add more people to the chat, click the Add people icon (), type the person's name, and click Add.
