Writing Knowledge Articles

Welcome to the Knowledge Article Guidelines. How are you? Make sure you have a comfortable seat while we walk through the knowledge article creation process. It shouldn't take too long. Take a break if you need to. Stretch.

We're going to take a journey that takes a different approach to writing content. Some writers get comfortable in their mastery of the template - the formulaic simplicity of a highly structured, monolithic manual. Contractions are fine. Embrace the bold. The sentence fragment.

When we write knowledge articles, we write for tasks that make a connection with our users. There's a flexibility for the personal. It's OK if it takes some re-learning, and un-learning to hone our muscle memory to write in a different way.
