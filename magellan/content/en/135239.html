Working with calendar events

Complete the following to work with the calendar:

  1. To open the calendar, click the calendar tab in the channel tabs. Typically, a department-wide calendar will be in the General channel.

  2. To add an event, do the following:

    1. Click in the cell for the start day of the even.

    2. Click +Add in the cell. The New Item page dialog box opens.

    3. Enter the information

    4. Click Save

  3. To view the details of an event, click on the event. The details page opens.

  4. To edit an event, do the following:

    1. Click on the event. The details page opens.

    2. Click the View tab and click Edit Item.

    3. Edit the details of the event as required.

    4. Click Save.
