Finding and sharing the link to a SharePoint site

Complete the following to find or share the link to a SharePoint site:

  1. (Optional) To find a SharePoint site from the main Office page, do the following:
    1. In your browser, go to www.office.com and log in when prompted.
    2. Scroll to the bottom of the page and locate the site under SharePoint.
    3. If the site is not listed under SharePoint, click Go to SharePoint. The SharePoint home page opens.
    4. Find the SharePoint site on the page or search for the site using the Search bar.
  2. (Optional) To share the site URL, do the following:
    1. Open the SharePoint site.
    2. Copy and send the URL.
